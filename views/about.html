ABOUT US
The Testino family first step foot on American soil on March 12 1962. When they Immigrated from there hometown of Corrado located in Bari Italy .Bringing with them nothing but there Recipes.
In August of 1965 Donna's Pizza Opened its doors. In Palisades Park NJ
Over a Half a Century later (The Famous Donna's Pizza) Is still serving the public today.

Second Generation


Is now taking their family's legacy to new heights and eventually nationwide. As numerous locations have already opened up throughout the State of New Jersey.
